Share your creations and earn rewards when other players purchase them.

<color=yellow>Turn your build into something others can use.</color>
1. Save your Blueprint using the <color=orange>Plan Hammer</color>.
2. Use the <color=orange>PUBLISH</color> tab to view local blueprints.
3. Set a price to your blueprint.
4. Press <color=orange>Publish</color> to make it available on the server.

Once published, your blueprint can be discovered and purchased by other players.

<color=yellow>Collect your earnings when you're ready</color>
1. Press <color=orange>Collect All</color> to receive your rewards or select a specific blueprint and press <color=orange>Collect</color> to receive the purchase order once.
2. Your ledger will update once the payout is claimed.
