The construction ward enables to materialize ghost pieces.

1. Check the <color=orange>PIECES</color> tab to view ghost pieces within range
2. Press the <color=orange>Build</color> button to begin construction
3. If you wish to skip pieces, interact with the list of pieces and hit <color=orange>Disable</color>

Fill the construction ward with all the materials required to build the ghost pieces.

1. Check the <color=orange>STATIONS</color> tab to view connected crafting stations
2. If the required station needed to build the piece is not connected, it will not build
3. If the required materials are not in the ward, it will not build
4. The requirements for the pieces includes the content of the piece (i.e. containers with items, or, item stands with attached items)
